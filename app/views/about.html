  • In the beginning...

    March 2012

    Craig joined a dating site to meet some people. Nicole joined the same site at the urging of her co-workers. Neither was looking for anything serious.

    After each had been on the site for a few months, Nicole sent Craig a message with some general pleasantries. After about a week of online bantering, they decided to meet up, even though Nicole was a die hard OSU fan and Craig a Notre Dame fan.

  • The first date.

    March 10th, 2012

    The plan was to meet up at the Hey Hey Bar for some drinks and sourkraut balls. Seeing that the place was shoulder to shoulder hipsters, they decided to try out Hal & Al's.

    A few drinks later, it was time for a late night snack, so they made their way to Plank's. There they discovered the best pizza known to man.

    Craig's only regret was leaving the leftover pizza in Nicole's car.

  • Then Craig Moved In

    October 2012

    Craig had no doubt it was the right thing to do. Nicole was hesitant about toilet seats being left up and double the laundry. Looks like Craig was right on this one.
